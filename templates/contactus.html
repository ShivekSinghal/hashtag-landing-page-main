Response Times

We strive to respond to all inquiries in a timely manner. Our expected response times are as follows:

  • Emails: Within 24-48 hours during business days.
  • Phone Calls: Immediate response during business hours. Voicemails will be returned within 24 hours.
  • In-Person Visits: Immediate assistance during office hours.
  • Social Media Messages: Within 48 hours.

Enrollment

Enrollment-related inquiries will be handled by our enrollment coordinator. They will guide you through the process of signing up for classes, provide information on class availability, and assist with any enrollment issues.

Complaints and Feedback

We value your feedback and take complaints seriously. All feedback and complaints will be reviewed by the studio manager. We aim to resolve any issues promptly and effectively. You can expect a response within 48 hours of submitting your complaint.

Emergency Contact

In case of an emergency during class hours, please contact the main office immediately. We have protocols in place to handle emergencies swiftly and efficiently.

Privacy

All contact information provided to us will be kept confidential and used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiries. We do not share your contact information with third parties without your consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this "Contact Us" policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and the updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting.
